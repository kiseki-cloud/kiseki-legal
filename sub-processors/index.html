Sub-processors

Provided to active Customers on request

Kiseki uses a small set of vetted sub-processors to deliver the Service. Categories include:

  • Cloud infrastructure (compute, database, storage, edge / CDN);
  • AI inference providers (text generation, image generation, embeddings);
  • Transactional email delivery;
  • Merchant-of-record payment processing and tax compliance;
  • Video hosting (for the VSL Builder feature);
  • Public-data research tooling (no Customer PII);
  • Internal team productivity (email, calendar, docs — not used for Customer data).

All sub-processors handling Personal Data are bound by Data Processing Agreements with Standard Contractual Clauses where applicable. Encryption at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3) is enforced across all transfers.

How to request the canonical list

The full list of named sub-processors — vendor, purpose, data categories processed, region, and link to each sub-processor's DPA — is provided to active Customers as part of the Data Processing Agreement.

To receive the current list, email legal@thekiseki.app from the email address registered to your Kiseki account.

Change notification

Kiseki notifies Customers at least 30 days in advance of adding or changing sub-processors via:

  • Email to the primary admin on file;
  • Update to the canonical list provided under the DPA;
  • Customer has 30 days to object; if no resolution, Customer may terminate the affected portion of the Service.
